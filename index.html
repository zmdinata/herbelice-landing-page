Herbélice: Heritage in Every Scoop.

Experience the magic where the heritage of Nusantara spices blends with the smoothness of premium ice cream. A harmony of flavors that soothes the soul and indulges the palate.

Innovation from Nusantara's Heritage

Herbélice emerges as a bridge between Indonesia's rich jamu tradition and the luxury of premium ice cream. Inspired by ancestral recipes, we offer a unique flavor experience, bringing the warmth of Nusantara spices in an elegant, modern dessert. We are committed to quality: using selected natural ingredients from the finest spices, processed through cutting-edge hygienic methods, and made without artificial preservatives to ensure every scoop of Herbélice not only indulges the palate but also nourishes the soul.
